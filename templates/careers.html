Early Childhood Educator

  • Learn & Play
  • Cedar Falls, Iowa
Job Description

We are looking for a dedicated and nurturing Daycare Worker to join our team at Learn & Play. You will be responsible for providing a safe and stimulating environment for young children, promoting their social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development. You will work closely with other staff members to create a supportive and enriching atmosphere for children to learn and play.

Responsibilities
  • Supervise and ensure the safety of children at all times.
  • Plan and implement educational activities that promote children's development.
  • Assist with daily routines, including meal times, nap times, and bathroom breaks.
  • Communicate effectively with parents about their child's progress and needs.
  • Maintain a clean and organized environment.
Requirements
  • Previous experience working with children in a daycare or educational setting.
  • CPR and First Aid certification.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Patience, creativity, and a love for working with children.
  • High school diploma or equivalent; further education in child development is a plus.
