OUR MISSION

Learn & Play Preschool & Daycare proudly serves all of the Cedar Valley from our Cedar Falls location. Offering a safe, clean, educational, and fun environment for all children, we believe we are the best daycare and preschool in the Cedar Valley. We want you to come take a look!

We believe that all young people should be prepared to think deeply and creatively so that they have the chance to become the innovators, educators.

Our teachers are warm and nurturing, hold degrees in early education and childhood development coupled with years of experience, and are dedicated to helping your child reach his or her full potential.
